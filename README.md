# Constitution of a Living World

> Every choice leaves a mark.

An exploration of humanity's responsibility to each other, to the natural world, and to future generations.

This project seeks principles for a civilization that measures not only power and progress, but also the traces it leaves behind.

## Current Status

This repository is an open exploration.

It does not propose a final constitution.

It seeks to study existing global frameworks, identify their strengths and limitations, and explore whether humanity may benefit from a shared foundation of responsibility toward life, nature, and future generations.
